What’s the difference between ECN and STP accounts?

Both are straight-through models with no dealing desk interference, ensuring fair execution. The key difference is cost structure:
- STP: Slightly wider spreads but zero commission—clear, all-in pricing for most traders.
- ECN: Raw spreads close to 0 with a low fixed commission—favored by day traders and scalpers seeking the tightest pricing.
